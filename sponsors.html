MICHIGAN STATE UNIVERSITY WOMEN IN COMPUTING 2021-2022 ANNUAL REPORT

On behalf of MSU WIC, I would like to express our gratitude to all of our industry partners, the Computer Science and Engineering Department, the Women in Engineering Program, and the College of Engineering for their continued support this year. There are not enough words to express how much your support impacts and improves all of the programs and opportunities that MSU WIC offers its members. Your continued support during these uncertain times has not gone unnoticed and has allowed us to continue working towards our mission of empowering and encouraging young women to discover a love for computing.

We would not be able to be a part of the Grace Hopper Celebration for Women in Computing without you all. Your contributions have provided women in the CSE program at MSU the opportunity to be a part of an international community of empowered female technologists. This conference allows our members the opportunity to not only attend technical talks and expand their technical knowledge, but also network with other attendees and explore vast career opportunities. This year, we were able to sponsor 17 students to attend virtual GHC 2021. GHC 2022 has been announced as a hybrid event, and we look forward to attending.

Even outside of the Grace Hopper Conference, your contributions help us organize our weekly meetings. Our meetings include technical talks, resume workshops, Lean In circles, social events and more – all of which help our members develop various skills helpful after graduation. Ultimately, your generous contributions help make WIC what it is today – a source of friendship, support, and countless wonderful memories.

Additionally, we were able to host the second annual MSU WIC Career Fair in Fall 2021. This virtual event helped connect our members to employment opportunities with our industry partners. We look forward to partnering with The Center for Spartan Engineering to host this career fair again this fall.

MSU WIC partnered with the following technology leaders in offering these opportunities to students:

Gold: GM | Domino's | Quicken Loans

Silver: MSUFCU | Dow | BP | Atomic Object

Bronze: Target | Deloitte | General Mills | Elektrobit | Delta Dental

Thank you for everything! We look forward to future engagement opportunities with you, whether that is in-person or virtually!

Sincerely,

Samantha Sebestyen

2021 - 2022 MSU WIC Vice President
